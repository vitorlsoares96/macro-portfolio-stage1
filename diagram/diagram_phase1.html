Phase 1 — From indicator to action: full pipeline map

The 12 raw indicators (left) feed Module A (economic cycle nowcasting) and Module B (market risk). Each module synthesizes its indicators into a single score, which is classified into a category — regime (Module A) or risk level (Module B). Crossing the two classifications defines the recommended action, in the 4×3 matrix on the right. Hover over an indicator to see why it was chosen.
